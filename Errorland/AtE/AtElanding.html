After The End

Set in a post-apocalyptic earth, After the End, if all goes to plan, will eventually become a
series of five novels to be written and published by Error. Progress on the books is slow. This
is intentional. Being the subject of my hyperfixation for the majority of my life now, I am very
intentionally taking my time with these to make them everything I want them to be.

Originally conceived of as a webcomic, after nearly 3 years of work that particular project was
abandoned after In part due to burn out and in part due to my own sub-par art skills leading to
me not all that happy with the final product, this was ultimately abandoned and the format was
changed to that of a novel (a much more sustainable medium given my own creative strengths).

I’ve decided to keep the old comics up on this site. While I’ll be the first to admit it isn’t
very good, I put in far too much effort into it over the years to feel ashamed. It was, after
all, in part of what drove me to help found Author’s Anarchy in the first place, and it would
feel wrong to get rid of it. If nothing else it is around as a record of where it all began,
and perhaps a reminder to any other aspiring creatives out there, no one gets it perfect on
their first try.
