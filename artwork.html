What is this gallery?
Over the years since we started author's anarchy, much of the world seems
to have come to the conclusion that queer people along with almost every
other historically marginalized group are public enemy #1.
This made Error mad. Mad that people in power seem intent to blame every
problem they helped create on anyone else or more than happy to turn a
blind eye when others do.
As one lonely anarchist, there may not be much that error can do, but
this gallery is here to try. To say that no matter who you are, you are
always welcome on our silly little site, and that yes, you have a right
to be angry.
