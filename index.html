Week 156

The anarchists proudly present:
Authors' Anarchy 3.0!
Well, part of it at any rate. As it turns out, juggling coding your own website along with academia, work and our other creative pursuits is more difficult than you'd think, but hey, after over a year of work we finally have at least something to show for ourselves.
We’re still ironing out the kinks, working to make navigation easier, create new pages and finish porting everything over from the new site, but the nice thing about coding your own website mostly from scratch is that 100% of the bugs are your fault and thus, 100% of them can be fixed.
For now though, we’ll celebrate. One year of effort to build this site and to the two sites that came before it. Here’s to three years of anarchy, and many more to come.
Long live the revolution!
